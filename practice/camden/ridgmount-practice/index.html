About Ridgmount Practice

Ridgmount Practice is an NHS GP practice located in Camden, London. All patient registrations are managed through the NHS — there is no fee to register. Use the button above to start your registration online.

The practice is rated Good by the Care Quality Commission (CQC), the independent regulator for health and social care services in England.

According to the most recent NHS GP Patient Survey, 91.2% of patients reported a positive overall experience of care at this practice.

This practice is part of the CENTRAL CAMDEN Primary Care Network (PCN).

Frequently asked questions

How do I register with this practice?
You can register online at no cost via the NHS at gp-registration.nhs.uk, or call the surgery directly. You'll need proof of address and ID.
How do I contact the practice?
Call 020 73876306 during opening hours, or visit them at the address shown above.
What area does this practice serve?
This practice serves patients in Camden and surrounding postcodes in London. Practice catchment areas are set by the practice itself — call to confirm whether your address is in their boundary.
Where is the data on this page from?
Phone numbers and addresses come directly from the NHS Organisation Data Service (ODS) and are refreshed weekly. Patient survey scores come from the NHS GP Patient Survey. CQC ratings come from the Care Quality Commission.
