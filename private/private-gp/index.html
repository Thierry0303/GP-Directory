Private GPs in London offer same-day or next-day appointments, longer consultations (typically 20-30 minutes vs the NHS 10 minutes), and continuity with a single doctor. They handle the full range of general medical care — health checks, repeat prescriptions, travel vaccinations, sexual health screening and referrals to private specialists. Most operate on a pay-per-visit basis (£100-£250 per consultation) or via a registered membership model. Many are CQC-rated Good or Outstanding.
